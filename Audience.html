Audience

Our audience consists mainly of nuuii’s typical consumer. That is a female aged between 24-44 years old who fits in the millennial or X generation. She is an educated person and lives in an urban hub. Her nationality is Danish or Swedish and she is a part of the middle class. The goal is to engage other audience groups as well since the platforms use are visited by a broad range of people.

Objectives

-broadening the size of the community on social media
-targeting the desired audiences easily
-boosting the positive brand image and customer satisfaction
-enhancing brand awareness
-educating the audience about nuuii’s beliefs
-engaging the audience to interact with the brand
