SoMe Strategy

Setting our SoMe goals:

The first step of our strategy is to set our goals and be clear of what the benefits of creating a SoMe strategy are.

  1. Our main goal is to generate more sales for nuuii:

    We want to showcase our new products through social media and in that way increase the reach those products can have.

  2. Our second goal is to boost community engagement:

    Users nowadays respond to brands that are aware of their position in their community. When creating a loyal customer base we can rely on those customers to share our posts, leave reviews and create user generated content that promotes nuuii.

  3. Our third goal is to drive more traffic to the website:

    We want to create links to our website through sponsored content and also we can track how many conversions we get from socials to actual sales when using promoted content.

  4. Our fourth goal is to create a recognisable corporate style/image:

    Expanding the reach also means that people will look at our ads and have no idea what nuuii is. We want to create a cohesive style that can be easily remembered and recognised when they scroll through their feed. This also increases brand awareness.

Setting our target audience and their preferred social media channels:

We selected facebook, pinterest and instagram as our distribution channels because they fit our target group. We will describe more in depth in our SoMe categories so click the specific channel on our navbar to know more.

Target group:

  • Middle class
  • 24-44 years old 
  • Educated
  • Female
  • Gen x / Millennial
  • Lives in an urban hub
  • Nordic: Danish, Swedish

Keeping a cohesive social media theme:

We have shared in this e-document our brand guide and how to post on our different social media channels. We have included examples as well as the correct colors, our style is important as we discussed in our goals. The customer recognises brand colors and has a sense of familiarity when seeing cohesive content.

Posting at the correct times for each channel:

We want to ensure our community managers respond to people’s questions and read the reviews of our products. When posting in correct hours we also have a higher chance of engagement and therefore likes/comments. In our individual tabs we describe the best time to post for each distribution channel.

Our social media strategy calendar:

Keeping our posts scheduled and planned is important to the process of creating original and creative content. We included the calendar in our tab and it is easily accessible for further examination.
