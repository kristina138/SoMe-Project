Style

The main aim is to keep the same aesthetics and brand integrity across all platforms. We put emphasis on using visuals rather than text. The colorway is to be kept close to the original (the color palette of the website). The wiggle room is there to give our social media more dimension and not to appear flat at first sight when taking in the whole feed. This also helps with diversity of the content.

Through our content we try to convey the story of Nuuii. As the second priority for our feed comes content based around environment and sustainability. These are hard topics for some and can come off as preachy or depressing. Our approach is to be light and refreshing but still keeping Nuuii visual outline.

Tone
